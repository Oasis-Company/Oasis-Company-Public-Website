Oasis Company & Its Sub Orgs

Blocks

Each block represents an organization within the Oasis ecosystem. Select one to apply. All supervisory organizations act as guardians and firefighters — not administrators — within their domains.

How to Apply

Two Ways In

Choose the path that suits you best. Every application is reviewed by the community.

01
Form Template
Use our structured application form. Tell us who you are, what you bring, and which Block you want to join. This is the recommended path for most applicants.
02
Creative Free-Form
Break the mold. Write anything — a manifesto, a poem, a letter, a design, a screenshot of your vision. Creativity is the only requirement. Show us who you are.
